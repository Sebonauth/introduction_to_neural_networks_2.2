A neuron, the fundamental unit of the nervous system, consists of three main parts:

  • Dendrites: Branch-like structures that receive messages from other neurons.
  • Cell Body (Soma): The neuron's core that processes incoming signals.
  • Axon: A long fiber that transmits signals to other neurons or muscles.
